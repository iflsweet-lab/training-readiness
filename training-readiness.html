Composite Score Formula

A weighted composite of all inputs, producing a 0–100 readiness score:

S = (HRV_norm×0.28) + (Sleep_norm×0.22) + (Recovery_norm×0.18) + (Readiness_norm×0.15) + (Subjective_norm×0.10) + (Context_adj×0.07)

Each component is normalised 0–1 before weighting. Context adjustors (days since hard session, load status, sleep trend) apply multipliers.

HRV Composite (weight: 28%)

  • Morpheus morning HRV vs your 7-day baseline → % deviation score
  • Ōura overnight HRV average contributes secondary signal
  • >10% above baseline = strong positive; >10% below = strong negative
  • Acute-to-chronic ratio applied if baseline entered

Sleep Composite (weight: 22%)

  • AutoSleep Quality % (primary), Total minutes, Deep sleep minutes
  • Ōura Sleep Score cross-referenced
  • Deep sleep < 60 min penalises score regardless of total sleep
  • Trend (3-night) applies multiplicative penalty if "poor"

Recovery Composite (weight: 18%)

  • Athlytic Recovery Score directly normalised
  • Training load status: Optimal=1.0×, High=0.8×, Overreaching=0.5×

Readiness Composite (weight: 15%)

  • Ōura Readiness + AutoSleep Readiness averaged
  • Morpheus Score contributes 30% weighting here

Subjective (weight: 10%)

  • Self-reported wellbeing (1–10) and soreness (1–5, inverted)
  • Strong override: subjective ≤3 → forces Rest regardless of objective data

Context Adjustors (weight: 7%)

  • Days since last hard session: 0–1d = −10pts, 2d = neutral, 3+d = +5pts
  • VO2 Max declining → mild training encouragement (+3)
  • Resting HR > personal norm threshold → −8pts

Sleep Disruption Risk Model

Flag triggered when ≥2 of:

  • Sleep trend = poor OR AutoSleep quality < 65%
  • Score > 70 (temptation to train hard exists)
  • Days since hard session ≤ 1
  • Athlytic load = High or Overreaching
  • Deep sleep < 1.0h
